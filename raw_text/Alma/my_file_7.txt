Alma Founder and CEO Dr. Harry Ritter joins Cheddar News' Closing Bell to discuss the company's recent $50 million fundraise amid the worldwide increase in teletherapy. Alma currently operates in eight states, but it is planning to use the funds to expand further and bolster its online platform that helps therapists and patients alike. Alma can help you find a therapist that best fits your needs, in-network, and for a reasonable price.
